Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026
The short version: NFL Oracle does not sell your data. We collect minimal information needed to run the service. We use Google Analytics and may show Google ads, which have their own data practices described below.

1. Who We Are

NFL Oracle is an AI-powered NFL game prediction tool that uses machine learning, Elo ratings, and team statistics to generate game outcome probabilities. We are not affiliated with the NFL, any NFL team, or any sports betting company.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Email address — only if you voluntarily subscribe to our weekly best bets digest. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email we send.
  • Usage data — pages visited, predictions run, time on site. This is collected anonymously via Google Analytics.
  • Browser storage — we use your browser's localStorage to remember your team picks, dark/light mode preference, and notification settings. This data never leaves your device.
  • Push notification subscriptions — if you enable browser push notifications, we store your subscription endpoint to send alerts. You can revoke this at any time in your browser settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To send weekly best bets emails (subscribers only)
  • To send game alerts via push notifications (if enabled)
  • To improve the accuracy and features of the prediction model
  • To understand how users interact with the site (analytics)

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand site traffic and usage patterns. Google Analytics may collect your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and pages visited. This data is processed by Google in accordance with their Privacy Policy. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Google AdSense & Advertising

We may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing history and interests. This is governed by Google's advertising policies.

You can control ad personalization through:

  • Google Ad Settings
  • Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out
  • Your browser's cookie settings

6. Cookies

We use the following cookies:

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function (session management)
  • Analytics cookies — Google Analytics (can be opted out)
  • Advertising cookies — Google AdSense, used to show relevant ads

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

7. Data Retention

  • Email subscribers — retained until you unsubscribe
  • Push subscriptions — retained until revoked via browser settings
  • Prediction logs — stored for up to 12 months to track model accuracy
  • Analytics data — retained per Google Analytics default (26 months)

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Google — for Analytics and AdSense services
  • Resend — our email delivery provider, for sending weekly digests

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from emails at any time
  • Revoke push notification permissions via your browser

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

10. Children's Privacy

NFL Oracle is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify subscribers of significant changes via email. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data requests, contact us at: privacy@nfloracle.com

For general inquiries: nfloracle.com
